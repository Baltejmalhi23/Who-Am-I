My Culture and Religon







Culture

I am apart of the Punjabi culture. The punjabi culture is a culture spread all over the world originating in Punjab, India. The Punjabi culture has many celebrations including Vaisakhi, Diwali, Lohri, and Holi. These celebrations are usually very big and joyful events with lots of family and friends. My culture also has alot of cultural foods inculding aloo paranthas, samosas, lassi, roti, and dahl. Some of these foods are ate at cutural celebrations or other events, and some are ate on a regualr basis.

Religon

My religion is Sikhism. Sikh is a beautiful religion practiced all over the world by more than 25 million adherents. In the Sikh culture we go to the gurdwara which we go to worship our gods. Vaisakhi is the Sikh New Year festival it is one of the most important dates in the Sikh calendar. It marks the start of the Punjabi New Year, but it is also a day to celebrate 1699 which is the year when Sikhism was born as a collective faith. On the day every year there are parades called the nagar kirtan.
